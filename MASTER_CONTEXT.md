## Vision

Zyzz is a personal AI Chief of Staff.

Its goal is to help the user think, plan, organize and execute tasks by intelligently orchestrating multiple AI models and external tools.

The project prioritizes long-term maintainability over short-term speed.

## Principles

- Simplicity first.
- Modular architecture.
- AI-first development.
- Documentation before implementation.
- Everything repetitive should be automated.
- Human decisions should always be documented.
